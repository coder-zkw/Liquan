About function modules

Our core functional modules cover wireless communication, wired communication, protocol conversion, data exchange, main control platform and specific functions.

The types of functional modules include: Wi-Fi module, Zigbee module, switch module, main control module, power module, specific function module, etc., which can be widely applied to Layer 3 switches, PTN switches, industrial switches, core routers, EPON systems, EoC. Systems and other products.

Remarks: The functional modules developed by our R&D team can standardize, modularize and simplify high-end communication products, and at the same time embed the design concepts of simplicity and complexity into complex communication product development. Each manufacturer can choose our core functional modules according to their own research and development capabilities, so that they can launch high-end communication products in a short time.
