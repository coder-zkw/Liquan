Industrial ring network switch solution

The industrial ring network switch has a self-healing ring, and its ring network has the advantages of redundancy and reliability. A certain link on the ring network is disconnected, and the data forwarding on the network is not affected. The self-healing ring can be divided into two types: private ring and public ring according to the communication protocol; according to the network structure, it can be divided into: single ring, intersecting ring, chain ring, and double ring. It has seven characteristics: 1 network failure can be self-healing high reliability, can ensure smooth network communication; 2IEEE802.3/802.3d/802.3u/802.3x, storage conversion switching mode; 3 with broadcast storm suppression function, port chain Road alarm information, power failure information relay output function; 4 with 12~48VDC dual redundant power supply; 5 working temperature -40~+85°C (wide temperature); 6 metal casing, generally aluminum alloy casing; 7 protection grade IP40 with PoE+.

Our industrial ring network switches include: 7FE+3GE, 8GE+2GE, 7GE+3XG, 4GE+3GE+3XG.

Note: A normal Ethernet switch cannot be a ring network. Once the link is ringed, a broadcast storm will occur and the switch will not work properly.
Layer 2 network management switch solution

A Layer 2 network management switch is a switch that supports only the data link layer protocol and can be managed through the Web. On the page of the IP address specified by the switch, you can manage the switch such as VLAN division, port rate control, port aggregation, and mirror configuration.

Multi-optical port switch solution

Our multi-optical switch, the number of optical ports covers 8 light, 16 light, 24 light, the rate covers 100 megabytes, gigabit, 10 megabytes, and the management capability is fool-configurable and network management.

Fool-type configurable multi-function switch solution

Our fool-type configurable multi-function switch, the port number covers 2/4/5/8/10/16/24/28 ports, and the speed covers 100 megabytes, gigabits and 10 megabytes. It can be used to realize the working mode of ultra-long-distance 250-meter transmission, cross-device port isolation, video transmission, VLAN division, and optical port rate selection through the DIP switch.

L2+ managed switch solution

The L2+ managed switch is a network management switch that supports some Layer 3 functions. It is more powerful than a Layer 2 switch. On the page of the IP address specified by the switch, you can manage VLANs, port rate, port aggregation, mirroring, ERPS, and access security.

POE switch solution

At present, our PoE switch basically supports PoE+, supports 802.3af and 802.3at protocols, and has a maximum power of 36 watts per port, with power management mode. It covers all kinds of electrical port switches, all of which are designed with PoE and non-PoE.

Industrial Internet Gateway Solution

Our industrial internet gateway solution supports 6 Gigabit ports, RS232/485 serial ports, USB, Wi-Fi, ZigBee, LTE, PoE+ and other functions. It supports multiple IoT protocols and provides a rich IoT interface for easy connection. Various sensors and IoT devices are suitable for deploying edge analysis and filtering data to prevent massive amounts of unwanted data from flooding into the data center and the cloud.

Optical transceiver (Gigabit, 10 Gigabit)

The optical transceiver is a communication device for photoelectric medium conversion. Our company introduced the Gigabit optical transceiver in 2003 and the 10G optical transceiver in 2017. The optical transceiver designed by our company has the characteristics of stable performance and novel features.
