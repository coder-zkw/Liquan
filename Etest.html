Network teste:

The iTester series network tester supplied by our company covers test bandwidths: 10M, 100M, 1000M, 2.5G, 5G, 10G, 25G, 40G, 100G. It can test L2-L7 communication protocol and do protocol simulation. It is China's independent innovation. The product, with complete independent intellectual property rights, is an important part of our company's communication product testing program.

iTester series network tester can test fiber optic transceivers, SFP optical modules, switches, routers, G/EPON, EoC, DSLAM, PLC, MSTP and other communication products. According to the testing ability, it can be divided into: simple type, production type, research and development type, all-round type, four levels; according to the number of supported boards, the host can be divided into: 2 slot host, 6 slot host, 12 slot host The number of ports that the board can support is: 2 ports, 4 ports, 8 ports, 12 ports, 16 ports. The iTester series network tester is a comprehensive and efficient test system for test projects. It is well-known in the industry with Spirent, IXIA and Nustream, but it is more cost-effective. It is now the most leading network tester brand in China.

Digital Oscilloscope:

Digital oscilloscopes are an essential tool for any engineer who designs, manufactures, and repairs electronic equipment. In the daily work, electronic engineers use digital oscilloscopes with convenient operation and accurate measurement to observe various analog and digital signals, find problems, solve problems, and face the challenges of various design and debugging today. How to choose an instrument with excellent performance and reasonable price is a problem that every supervisor and engineer has to face.

As the leading digital oscilloscope design, production and sales manufacturer in China, it has launched five generations of digital oscilloscope products in more than ten years, and achieved the first in many domestic industries, such as the earliest 300MHz bandwidth desktop oscilloscope; the highest sampling rate is 2GSa. /s, digital oscilloscope with waveform capture rate of 2000wfms/s; firstly break the digital oscilloscope with 1M memory depth; bandwidth up to 1GHz, maximum sampling rate up to 5GSa/s, waveform capture rate of 180,000wfms/s, storage depth up to 140M Oscilloscopes and more. In order to meet the needs of different customers, RIGOL digital oscilloscope gradually formed several different series of DS1000E, DS1000D, DS1000CA, DS1000B and DS6000 to meet the needs of different users and help engineers improve test efficiency.
